Receiving Feedback and Complaints

Participants can provide feedback or make complaints through various channels (e.g., directly to staff, via email, through surveys). MCS may be contacted at:

  • Email: hello@mcsarmidale.com.au
  • Phone: 0491 716 968
  • Address: 31 Chestnut Avenue, Armidale, NSW 2350

Any person receiving our services, their family, friends, advocates, or representatives, and staff. An advocate may be assigned at the request of any relevant person. Participants will be informed that engagement with family, friends and chosen community will be supported.

Acknowledging and Documenting: Complaints will be acknowledged within two working days. All complaints will be documented, and the participant will be informed that their complaint has been received and is being investigated.

Investigation: Complaints will be thoroughly investigated to understand the root cause and potential resolutions. Relevant staff and management will be involved as necessary. MCS will comply with the MCS Privacy and Dignity Policy and Procedure, as well as adhere to relevant confidentiality legislation.

Resolution: MCS aims to resolve complaints within 15 business days. Once the investigation is complete, MCS will take appropriate actions to resolve the complaint. The participant will be informed of the outcome and any changes or improvements that will be implemented.

Review and Continuous Improvement: Feedback and complaints will be reviewed regularly to identify trends and opportunities for improving service delivery.

Escalating Complaints:

  • Internal Review: Request an internal review by a senior manager if dissatisfied with the resolution.
  • NDIS Commission: If unresolved or if the complaint needs to be made externally to MCS, complaints can be directed to the NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission. Phone: 1800 035 544 Website: www.ndiscommission.gov.au
